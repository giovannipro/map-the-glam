Map the GLAM

Visualizing the contributions of cultural institutions to Wikipedia

Recently, several cultural institutions (also called GLAMs-Galleries, Libraries, Archives, Museums) have released in Wikipedia and other Wikimedia projects a large amount of their collections with the aim to make more largely accessible. Within these online platforms, the content is released under the Creative Commons License Attribution-ShareAlike and is shared by both the Wikipedia community and casual users. Through the use of visual representation of data, Map the GLAM attempts to make cultural institutions aware of their impact on the Wikimedia projects. In specific, the project focuses on the ETH Bibliothek, one of the leading libraries in the world in terms of images uploaded in Wikimedia Commons.
