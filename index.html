In the world, several cultural institutions yearly upload thousands of pictures of their collection to Wikipedia under free license.

Every single image incorporates plenty of data about its technical features and its use on Wikipedia and related sister projects.

Map the GLAM visualizes the collection of the ETH library, a world leading cultural institution in terms of contributions to Wikipedia.
